# Content Calendar — May 2026

## Themes (monthly anchors)

1. **AI-generated UI and the a11y debt it creates** — v0, Bolt, Lovable, Claude Artifacts: what WCAG criteria they most commonly break and what teams can do about it. Primary research anchor: accessibility-ai domain.
2. **Cognitive accessibility in practice** — moving past the staged "Cognitive Load & Neurodiversity" post into the broader discipline: COGA, WCAG 2.2 SCs 3.3.7–3.3.9, reading-age targets for UI copy.
3. **Automated scanning: what axe-core misses** — the gap between tooling confidence and real-world WCAG compliance; emerging scanners (Pa11y, IBM Equal Access, accessibility-insights); how AI scanners compare.
4. **ARIA evolution and new native HTML patterns** — popover API, `<dialog>`, `inert` attribute, ARIA 1.3 updates. Developer-focused.
5. **Screen reader UX research** — NVDA/JAWS/VoiceOver behavior gaps; reproducible patterns where AI improves (or hurts) screen reader UX.

> **Excluded topic:** "Cognitive Load & Neurodiversity" — already staged (2026-05-02, status: Ready on all platforms). No slot in this calendar repeats that specific topic.
