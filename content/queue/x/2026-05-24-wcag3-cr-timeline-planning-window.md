## 1

the W3C AG Working Group committed in April 2026 to publish a WCAG 3.0 timeline. three independent analyses now converge on the same window: Candidate Recommendation 2026-2027. final Recommendation no earlier than 2028-2030. WCAG 2.2 AA is still the operative legal standard.

if you are building tooling that maps against WCAG 3.0 outcomes today, the 12-18 month runway to CR is the planning window. not the 174-outcome March 2026 WD. many of those outcomes will be dropped before CR. build against the structure, not the count.
